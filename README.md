**3IN1 POPULAR FOOD**

This project is is about a start-up restaurant website that provides three countriesâ€™ popular food in one place. We provide Indian, Chinese, and American popular cuisine in one place. On this website, customers can choose a variety of food, and order breakfast, lunch, dinner, and desserts. This website gives you options to reserve a table and pre-order for dine-in, Online Order for delivery, or to-go and book a restaurant for their special occasions. This system allows customers to modify the existing order or choose delivery for up to the next day. Customers also can customize orders to their choice. We also provide ingredients for each item on the menu and provide safety measures we have taken.
